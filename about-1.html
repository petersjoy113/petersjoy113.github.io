Our Story

About Staying Close

Staying Close is a daily devotional website built on one simple conviction: that drawing near to God is not reserved for church Sundays or spiritual seasons. It is a daily decision, a daily discipline, and a daily gift available to every believer who reaches for it.

This site exists to help you reach for it — every single day.

"Draw nigh to God, and he will draw nigh to you."

— James 4:8, KJV

What We Believe

We believe the King James Bible is the preserved Word of God, and that its language — though old — carries a weight and beauty that still speaks directly to the human heart. Every verse featured on this site comes from the KJV, paired with original reflections written to help you sit with the Word rather than rush past it.

We believe that a relationship with God is not built in grand gestures alone, but in the quiet, consistent moments: a verse read in the morning, a reflection carried through the day, a prayer whispered before sleep. These small acts of nearness add up to a life genuinely lived close to the Father.

What You Will Find Here

Each day, Staying Close offers a featured verse from the King James Bible alongside an original devotional reflection — not copied from any book or website, but written freshly to meet you where you are. You will also find a growing library of verses organized by topic, so that whether you are walking through fear, grief, waiting, joy, or doubt, there is a word here that speaks to your season.

Our topics include peace, strength, hope, trust, love, healing, forgiveness, patience, renewal, purpose, and more — because the Christian life touches all of these things, sometimes all in the same week.

A Word About This Site

Staying Close is an independent Christian website. It is not affiliated with any denomination, church, or ministry organization. It is simply the work of a believer who wanted to create a quiet, beautiful place on the internet where people could come to be reminded of what matters most.

Every reflection you read here is original writing. Every verse is from the public domain King James Version. There are no paywalls, no subscription fees, and no requirement to create an account. The Word of God has always been free, and access to it here will remain so.

Our Prayer for You

That you would leave this site even one degree closer to the Father than when you arrived. That a verse would stay with you through a difficult afternoon. That a reflection would put words to something you have been feeling but could not name. That in the middle of your ordinary day, you would sense that you are not alone — that He is near, that He is attentive, and that He has not stopped thinking about you.

Welcome to Staying Close. We are glad you are here.
